Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow,your taste buds with festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet Caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Ingredients


For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teastpoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oli,plus more for the pan
    •

    For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 onces brie,cut into cubes(scape rind off with a paring knife if desired,though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
    •

    Method

    1. Make the Focaccia dough
      In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough Ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dought,thought I somentimes kneaed it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
